Courses Offer

B.Com(Bachelor of Commerce)

A general undergraduate program in commerce, B.Com. provides a solid foundation in subjects like accounting, finance, economics, business law, taxation, and auditing. This course is often considered a stepping stone for further studies like M.Com, CA, or MBA. Graduates typically pursue careers in finance, accounting, banking, or business management.

Duration: 3 Years.




BBA(Bachelor of Business Administration)

BBA is a popular course that focuses on developing business and management skills. It includes subjects like marketing, human resource management, organizational behavior, financial management, and entrepreneurship. This program helps students understand the principles of business administration and prepares them for leadership roles in the corporate world.

Duration: 3 Years
