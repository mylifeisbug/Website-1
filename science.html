Courses offers



B.Sc. In Computer Science

This undergraduate program focuses on the fundamentals of computer science, programming languages, algorithms, data structures, artificial intelligence, machine learning, and software development. Students also get a hands-on approach with coding practices and real-world applications of computing technology. It is an ideal course for those interested in pursuing a career in the IT sector, software development, data science, or research.

Duration: 3 Years

B.Sc. in Biotechnology



This program is designed to impart knowledge about biological processes, molecular biology, genetic engineering, bioinformatics, and industrial biotechnology. Students study the applications of biological systems in agriculture, healthcare, and environmental science. Graduates can work in biotechnology research, pharmaceuticals, environmental sustainability, or in agricultural sectors.

Duration: 3 Years
