Courses Offer

BA in English Literature

A BA in English Literature allows students to explore classic and contemporary works of literature from around the world. The program includes the study of poetry, drama, fiction, and literary theory. It enhances critical thinking, analytical abilities, and communication skills, making graduates well-suited for careers in writing, publishing, journalism, teaching, and content development.

Duration: 3 Years



BA in Psychology

A BA in Psychology focuses on understanding human behavior, mental processes, and emotional development. Students learn about cognitive psychology, clinical psychology, behavioral psychology, and social psychology. This course opens doors to careers in counseling, social work, human resources, mental health, and research.

Duration: 3 Years
